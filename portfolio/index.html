Video Games

My love for video games is what peaked my curiosity to learn about computers and technologies. To add to the curiosity is also my competiveness. The drive to be at the top of the leaderboards or ranking systems proved that my commitment and efforts allowed me to be in the top percentile. Career Interests

My career interests began from wanting to be a game developer, but that changed into Big Data. Finding and building solutions to harness data is difficult, but is made possible by an abundant and ever-growing set of technologies such as cloud computing. Cloud computing has many components with each their own set of responsibilites and are used together to build complex data solutions. All of these have caught my attention and my curiosity to learn and put the technologies to use.

Interests

My biggest interest is video games and the technologies and designs that are implemented to create them. With this I am trying to build a game engine from scratch. I also have interests in artificial intelligence and machine learning because of J.A.R.V.I.S. or F.R.I.D.A.Y. in Marvel's Iron Man movies. Although, I have not taken steps to start learning about these topics, but I know I will pick these topics up to learn in the future. And possibly pursue a master's degree in one these fields.
